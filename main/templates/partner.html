Introduction

ENDROID Reseller Partner Program offers all the marketing & sales tools, pre-sales & technical support and necessary training, to educate our Resellers how to market and resell our products and drive customer adoption. This page describes all the elements of the our Reseller Program and the value it brings you, our partner. You will also find information on what is expected from a partner and how to join the program

Market

According to the title report, India CCTV Market Outlook, 2027-28”, the India CCTV market is anticipated to attain market size of more than INR 27000 Crore.

Closed Circuit Television (CCTV) commonly known as video surveillance has long admittance into the people lives and their application in supervision over vulnerable areas is increasing notably in India. The Indian CCTV market is still relatively young as it being widely used in various applications throughout the regions. In recent years, there has been a tremendous growth in the use of CCTV to prevent crime in public space.

Advantages

Industry-leading Products and Technology

ENDROID innovation focuses on delivering an outstanding customer experience, developing industry-leading products and technology, developing best-in-class partnerships, and building the best team in the industry.

Attractive Partner Margins

We works with a so-called recommended Retail Price List for its products. Based on this Retail Price List, we offers its partners an attractive margin. Next to the margin on the products itself, the partner also benefits from margin on installation and accessories.

Partner Portal

our Partner Portal allows you to check prices online , Download marketing material & softwares for installation as well as service supports.

Brand Marketing

We will develop brand Marketing activities to stimulate customer demand and increase the awareness of our Products. These activities include (but are not limited to): Trade shows, seminars and exhibitions, PR activities, Ad campaign on Whatsapp & other social media ,Web, Google etc.

Partners will benefit from the leads that will be generated via these brand marketing activities.

Marketing Materials

We offers partners a rich set of marketing materials, e.g. product brochures, videos, presentations, high resolution pictures that partner can use to market and sell the our Products. Each new partner receives a standard set of these marketing materials free of charge as part of the Partner (Sales) Toolkit.

Technical Support

We provides phone and email direct support to all the customers, which makes things easy for partners. Dedicated Technical Support for our Reseller Partners

Dedicated Partner Account Manage

Our Reseller Partners are assigned a Partner Account Manager. The Partner Account Manager is the first point of contact for all our related queries and helps drive the sales.

How to Join?

The first step in becoming a Our Reseller is filling an online registration form. We will then go through all the aspects of becoming a partner and share with you details around pricing, internal use discounts and legal aspects.

We value new partners, and is why this is a non-automated process, which is being led by members of the Management Team personally.
